Charging Tips

  • Most fast chargers accept credit cards and RFID cards
  • Download the PlugShare app for real-time availability
  • Plan your route with multiple charging options
  • Check connector compatibility before arriving

Charging Speeds

  • Fast (50kW+): 200-300km in 30-40 minutes
  • Standard: 200-300km in 4-6 hours
